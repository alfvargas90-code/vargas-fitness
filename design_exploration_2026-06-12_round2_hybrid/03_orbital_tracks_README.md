# Hybrid·03 — Three Orbital Tracks  (Day Arc + domains as flows)

**One-line POV:** "Your day in flow" — the three domains become three ribbons running dawn→night, a cinematic shape on top, exact numbers underneath.

## The fusion
This leans into Day Arc's cinematic side and folds Pillars in as **three time-tracks** instead of three columns. Recovery (cyan), Activity (orange), and Body (violet) each flow left→right across the day: Recovery dips after a compromised morning, Activity climbs through midday, Body sits as a single morning marker. A sun anchors dawn, the real moon anchors night, and a glowing NOW line crosses all three with a value-dot on each.

Below the visualization, the same three domains appear as **horizontal readout rows** with status pills (Compromised / In Motion / Steady) and the exact numbers — so the beautiful flow is always backed by precise data.

Time is the primary axis; domains are the moving parts.

## Best for
The user who wants the day to *feel* like a story and a system at once — a glanceable emotional read (where am I trending) plus a full data backstop one row down. The most "wallpaper-worthy" of the three.

## Trade-off
The flowing tracks are interpretive — they imply trends the underlying data only samples a few times a day, so the curves are partly illustrative. Gorgeous, but the least literal; pair it with the readout rows (as done here) so it never overstates what was actually measured.
