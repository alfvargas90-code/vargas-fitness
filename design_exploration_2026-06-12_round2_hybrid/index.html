02 — Time × Domain Matrix

You asked what new options the combination surfaces — the matrix is the one that only exists because you merged them. It's the only layout where the structure itself tells the truth about your data: recovery is a morning fact, body is a once-daily snapshot, activity accrues at midday, night is forecast. The empty cells are the feature. That's the System Architect's dashboard.

If you want a daily driver over a console, 01 Arc-Crowned Pillars is the pragmatic pick — same glance-everything grid, now time-aware. 03 Orbital Tracks is the most beautiful but the least literal (the flows are partly illustrative). My move: ship 01 as the default view and build 02 as a "Day" tab for when you want to study the system.
