# Direction 1 — Hero-First Reading  ★ Claude's pick

**File:** `direction-1-hero-first.html`

## The user moment it serves
The **morning glance.** Alfredo opens the app once a day and wants the answer, not an
assignment. This layout makes the app do the deciding: an engine auto-selects the single
strongest current signal and renders it as one giant top card — today that's the
**approaching Jupiter return** (Vedic / Moon Mahadasha–Venus). The other three systems
collapse to one-line rows he can still tap open, so nothing is lost — but the default state
is *one answer, one move.*

## Why it fits Alfredo specifically
- His documented #1 growth area is **overanalysis / delayed execution.** Four parallel
  horoscope cards invite exactly the comparison-shopping that stalls him. Hero-First
  removes the choice: the system commits, he reads, he acts.
- Keeps the **moon orbital hero** he loves as the literal top of the screen.
- The auto-pick logic is honest — a small "auto-picked" tag shows *why* this system was
  surfaced, so it never feels like a black box.

## How the engine picks (mock logic)
Surfaces whichever system has the most time-sensitive live trigger. Priority order used here:
imminent return/ingress (Jupiter return ~Jul 12) > Da Yun / dasha shift > exact transit >
profection change > lunar phase. Falls back to lunar phase on quiet days.

## Aesthetic notes
Deep blue/purple gradient, starfield, moon orbital hero with three rings, glassy frosted
hero card with a pink wash, pink/coral italic narrative prose, gold "today's move" footer,
violet/cyan/gold pill labels. Pulled straight from the kids-astro + timing-weather DNA.

## Chart data surfaced
12th-house Sagittarius profection year · Jupiter return ~Jul 12 2026 · Venus antardasha
(first relationship-warm sub-period) · Sade Sati pressure on foundations · the Capricorn /
Saturn embodiment year opening Aug 30.

## Tradeoff
Hides depth by default. If Alfredo's actual daily habit is to read *all* systems every day,
this fights that — Direction 2 or 4 serve that better.
