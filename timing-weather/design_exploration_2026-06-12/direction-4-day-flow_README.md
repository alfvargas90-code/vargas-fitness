# Direction 4 — Narrative Day-Flow  (solar aesthetic)

**File:** `direction-4-day-flow.html`

## The user moment it serves
The **daily ritual** — the most literal answer to the product's name, *timing weather.* The
sun rides a dawn→dusk day-arc at the top (with a "Now · 1:18 PM" marker), then the day reads
top-to-bottom as a vertical timeline grounded in real hours: morning Moon-void → midday
Jupiter (you-are-here) → afternoon profection frame → evening Venus → night Saturn. Each beat
carries the relevant system and a single concrete "do" line.

## Why it fits Alfredo specifically
- Turns abstract astrology into a **schedule he can act on** — "don't launch before 2:40 PM,"
  "reach out tonight," "bank the fire, don't spend it." Action-oriented, which is how he wants
  to operate once a call is made.
- The time-of-day spine weaves all four systems together without making him pick a tab —
  convergence as a story, not a comparison.
- A "you are here" gold pulse means the app meets him wherever he opens it.

## Structure
A gradient timeline rail (cyan→gold→orange→coral→violet) with glyph nodes; the current beat
gets a pulsing gold node + gold-washed card. The background deepens toward night. Closes with
a "tomorrow's first signal" whisper (the New Moon in 3 days) for return-tomorrow pull.

## Aesthetic notes
Canonical solar look — near-black→night gradient, sun on a day-arc, gold EXPANSION headline,
glassy beat cards, gold forecast headlines, coral italic emphasis in the prose, gold "↳ do"
lines. Reuses the live dashboard's card + heading system.

## Chart data surfaced
Moon void window · applying Jupiter→Sun + 30-days-to-return · 12th-house profection → Capricorn
year Aug 30 · Venus antardasha (soften the self-sufficient posture) · Sade Sati + Bing-Wu
peer-fire burnout caution · New Moon in 3 days.

## Tradeoff
It's a scroll, not a glance, and the five-beat narrative is the most authored of the four — so
it leans hardest on good daily prose generation. If the writing engine is strong, this is the
one people fall in love with; if it's weak, this direction exposes it most.
