# Direction 4 — Narrative Day-Flow  (Claude's close second)

**File:** `direction-4-day-flow.html`

## The user moment it serves
The **daily ritual.** This is the most literal answer to the product's name — *timing
weather.* Instead of system-by-system, the day is read top-to-bottom as a vertical timeline
grounded in actual hours: morning Moon-void → midday Jupiter (you-are-here) → afternoon
profection frame → evening Venus → night Saturn. Each beat carries the relevant system and a
single concrete "do" line.

## Why it fits Alfredo specifically
- Turns abstract astrology into a **schedule he can act on** — "don't launch before 2:40 PM,"
  "reach out tonight," "bank the fire, don't spend it." Action-oriented, which is how he
  wants to operate once a call is made.
- The time-of-day spine naturally weaves all four systems together without making him pick a
  tab — convergence presented as a story, not a comparison.
- A "you are here" pulse on the current beat means the app meets him wherever he opens it.

## Structure
A gradient timeline rail (cyan→violet→pink→coral) with glyph nodes. The current beat is
highlighted with a pink pulsing node + pink-washed card. Closes with a "tomorrow's first
signal" whisper (the New Moon in 3 days) to create return-tomorrow pull.

## Aesthetic notes
Deep blue/purple gradient deepening toward the bottom (day → night), moon orbital hero,
day-arc progress bar, glassy beat cards, pink/coral italic prose, gold "↳ do" lines,
serif/sans mix from the kids-astro DNA.

## Chart data surfaced
Moon void-of-course window · applying Jupiter→Sun + 30-days-to-return · 12th-house profection
→ Capricorn year Aug 30 · Venus antardasha (soften the self-sufficient posture) · Sade Sati +
Bing-Wu peer-fire burnout caution · New Moon in 3 days.

## Tradeoff
It's a scroll, not a glance — and the five-beat narrative is the most editorial/authored of
the four, so it leans hardest on good daily prose generation. If the writing engine is weak,
this direction exposes it most. If the writing is strong, this is the one people fall in love
with.
