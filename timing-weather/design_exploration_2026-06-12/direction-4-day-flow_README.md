# Direction 4 — Command Center

**File:** `direction-4-day-flow.html` · best viewed at iPad/desktop width (~1024px)

## What it is
The maximalist direction that **fuses both references into one hero**: reference 1's four
corner gauges AND reference 2's orbital planets, around a single sun, with EXPANSION across
the center. Below it the densest grid of the four — everything visible at once:

- **Row A (3-col):** Daily Reading · Event Radar · Today's Insight
- **Row B (2-col):** Upcoming Conditions · Recommended Actions
- **Row C (4-col):** Sky Conditions · Planet Influences · Top Drivers · Why + Confidence
- NOW strip between hero and grid

## Why it exists
You said to use **both** references as targets — this is the literal fusion: gauges (ref-1)
and orbit (ref-2) in the same hero, then the most information per screen. For when the goal is
a single "mission control" view rather than a focused read.

## Aesthetic
Gold sun with orbital planet glyphs + four conic corner gauges, gold EXPANSION title, glassy
cards, SVG Event Radar, colored stat bars and deltas — same tokens as the other three.

## Tradeoff
Densest and busiest of the four. The hero is doing the most (gauges + orbit + title overlap),
so it rewards a big screen; on a phone it would need to break into stacked sections.

## Chart data
Full set: gauges, orbital influences, consensus, Daily Reading, Event Radar, Upcoming,
Recommended, Sky, Planet Influences, Top Drivers, Expansion Score 78, Confidence.
