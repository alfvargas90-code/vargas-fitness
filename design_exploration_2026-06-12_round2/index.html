★ Claude's recommendation

01 — Hero-First (with a Pillars-style lower deck)

It's the most decision-useful of the four and the directest answer to circling: it pre-picks the one number that matters today (−4.2, Compromised), reframes it as guidance in your own voice, and demotes everything else to a calm ledger. Pillars is the runner-up — steal its "whole system visible, no hidden tabs" idea for Hero-First's lower section and you get focus and full visibility in one screen. Day Arc and Story are the more narrative, read-through takes — beautiful, but built for a morning sit-down, not a 5-second glance.
